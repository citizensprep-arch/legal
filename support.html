Common questions

I paid but my features are locked.

Open the app, go to the unlock screen, and tap “Restore Purchase.” Your one-time purchase will reactivate. Still stuck? Email us and we'll sort it out.

Is my information private?

Yes. Your answers stay on your device, no account is required, and photos of your form are used only to read it and are never saved. See our Privacy Policy.

Which interviews does it cover?

The U.S. naturalization (N-400) interview, including the civics and English tests, plus practice tracks for the green card (I-485) and removal-of-conditions (I-751) interviews.

How do I change the language?

Open the app, tap the “Me” tab, and choose your language. CitizensPrep is available in 10 languages.

How do I reset my passcode?

On the lock screen, tap “Forgot passcode?” — this removes the lock only. Your study progress is kept.
