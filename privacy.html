Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

CitizensPrep helps you prepare for your U.S. naturalization (citizenship) interview. Because the information involved is personal, we keep this simple and honest.

The short version

  • Your answers and progress are saved on your device, not on our servers.
  • We do not sell or share your personal information, and we don't show ads.
  • When you use an AI feature, only the text or photo for that feature is sent securely to our AI provider to create a response — it is not used for advertising or sold.
  • You can erase everything at any time.

What the app uses

  • Information you enter: your first name, state, interview date, chosen language, and — only if you use "My Application" — answers from your Form N-400 (such as trips, marriage, employer, and the honesty questions). Your A-Number is optional and only kept on your device if you enter it.
  • Your study progress: which questions you've practiced, your streak, and practice scores.
  • We do not ask for your Social Security number, and you do not need to create an account to study.

Where it is stored

All of the above is stored locally on your device. It stays there unless you use an AI feature (below). We do not keep a copy on our servers.

AI features

Some features use artificial intelligence: the Practice Interview, the Ask AI assistant, the "Check my answers" review, and the N-400 photo scanner. When you use one of these, the specific information needed for that feature — for example your typed question, your N-400 answers, or the photo you scan — is sent over a secure connection to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate the response you asked for. It is used only to produce that response. Photos you scan are sent only to read the text and are not saved by the app after the form is filled in. Please avoid entering information you do not need to share, and remember the AI gives general help, not legal advice.

Your choices

  • Erase all your data anytime with "Reset all progress" in the Me tab, or by deleting the app.
  • "My Application" is optional — you can leave it blank and still use everything else.

Children

CitizensPrep is intended for adults preparing for naturalization and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes & contact

If we change this policy, we'll update the date above. Questions? Email support@citizensprep.com.
